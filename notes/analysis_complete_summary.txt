KEY FINDINGS
1. Antibiotic Controls Worked:
   - Tetracycline: 9 significantly different taxa
   - Terra-Pro: 4 significantly different taxa
   - Cluster together in PCoA (similar antimicrobial effect)

2. ASx Lysate Had Comparable Effect:
   - ASx lysate: 1 significantly different taxon (DESeq2)
   - 0 significant taxa (ANCOM-BC2)
   - Microbiome composition similar to untreated controls

3. Caging Affects Microbiome:
   - Hive-reared bees cluster separately from caged controls
   - Environment influences community structure beyond colonization

4. Comb Successfully Seeded Microbiome:
   - 50-62% of caged bee gut bacteria came from comb
   - Validates experimental design for controlled establishment

5. Hive Environment Matters:
   - Hive-reared: 91.7% taxa overlap with comb
   - Caged: 58.1% taxa overlap with comb
   - Same taxa, different abundances = environment shapes development
